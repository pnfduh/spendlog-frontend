What SpendLog does

Everything you spent,
exactly when you spent it.

📅

Spending Calendar

See your spending mapped to dates with a visual heat map. Heavier spending days glow brighter. Tap any day to see every transaction.

⚡

Real-Time Alerts

The moment your bank posts a transaction, you get a push notification. No refresh, no manual import — it just appears.

Apple Pay Detection

Transactions made with Apple Pay flow through your bank and are automatically tagged. SpendLog knows it was Apple Pay even though no app can access it directly.

🏦

All Your Banks

Connect checking, savings, and credit cards from 12,000+ banks via Plaid. Your credentials never touch SpendLog's servers.

🔒

Bank-Grade Security

256-bit encryption, read-only bank access, and zero storage of credentials. Your money stays in your bank — SpendLog only sees the receipts.

📱

Installs Like a Native App

No App Store required. Add SpendLog to your home screen in two taps and it runs fullscreen with push notifications, just like a native app.

How it works

Up and running in 2 minutes.

01

Install the app

Tap "Add to Home Screen" below. SpendLog installs directly from your browser — no App Store, no download, no waiting.

02

Connect your bank

Tap the Accounts tab and link your bank via Plaid's secure OAuth flow. Takes under 60 seconds. SpendLog gets read-only access — never write access.

03

Spend normally

Pay with your card, Apple Pay, whatever you use. SpendLog detects it, logs it to your calendar, and sends you a notification instantly.
